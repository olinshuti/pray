Faith Foundations – Preview
Quick Overview

Faith Foundations is a 6-week journey designed to help you understand and apply the essential beliefs of Christianity. Each week covers a core doctrine with practical steps for daily living.

  • 6 weekly sessions with key Bible passages
  • Reflection questions for personal or group study
  • Simple prayers to strengthen your faith
  • Action steps for real-life application
Perfect for: New believers, small groups, or anyone wanting to revisit the basics of Christian faith.

"Now faith is the assurance of things hoped for, the conviction of things not seen."

Hebrews 11:1

  • Daily readings from the Gospel of John
  • Thought-provoking reflection questions
  • Prayer prompts for personal growth
  • Ideal for individual or group study
Perfect for: Anyone seeking to know Jesus more deeply and apply His teachings.

Fruit of the Spirit - Preview
Quick Overview

Fruit of the Spirit explores the nine attributes described in Galatians 5 and provides practical ways to cultivate them in your daily walk with God.

  • In-depth look at each fruit: love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, self-control
  • Personal reflection and application
  • Scripture readings and prayers
  • Growth challenges for each attribute
Perfect for: Anyone wanting to grow in Christlike character and spiritual maturity.

"But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, self-control..."

Galatians 5:22-23
